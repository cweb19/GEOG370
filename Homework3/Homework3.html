For the raster layer, I used OpenStreetMap on QGIS. For the vector layer, I used shapefile data from the Carrboro website, linked below.

I made two points demarcating Phillips Hall and Carolina Hall on UNC's campus, two lines highlighting the locations of East Rosemary St and Martin Luther King Jr Boulevard, and a polygon showing the location of Coker Arboretum on campus.

I used the "Places in Carrboro" shapefile found at this link: Shapefile Data
